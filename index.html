Why it matters now

The cybersecurity skills gap is a measurement problem.

Not a training problem. Not a tooling problem. You cannot hire what you cannot measure — and certifications don't measure reasoning.

Hiring is slow and inconsistent

Security teams typically run 3–5 technical rounds per candidate. Same person, different outcomes depending on who interviewed. Senior engineer time burns either way.

Certifications test recall, not reasoning

Real incidents demand judgment under uncertainty — something a multiple-choice exam cannot measure. A CISSP tells you someone studied. It doesn't tell you how they'd triage at 2am.

Attack surface keeps expanding

Cloud, SaaS, APIs, supply chains. Your team needs to reason through novel attack paths every week — not recite frameworks. Practical reasoning is the differentiator.

A bad senior hire is expensive

All-in, a bad senior security hire — salary, onboarding, replacement — can run into tens of lakhs in India or six figures elsewhere. Defensible evaluation is no longer optional.

What is ThreatReady

The platform that measures real attack reasoning.

The term we keep using

Attack reasoning is the ability to read an architecture, trace a probable attack path, and make defensible containment and mitigation decisions under pressure — without needing to recite a framework first.

FAQ

Questions we hear.

How is this different from Hack The Box, TryHackMe, or a CTF?

Hack The Box and TryHackMe teach hands-on exploitation through long-form labs and CTF challenges. Both are excellent learning platforms.

ThreatReady solves a different problem: can a candidate reason through an attack chain in 30 minutes, the way a senior interviewer would ask them to? Adaptive AI questions, MITRE ATT&CK mapped architectures, and a transparent rubric. It's not a replacement for learning — it's a replacement for technical phone screens.

Can candidates game the assessment with AI?

Copy-paste is disabled on every answer input. Each question is generated by AI based on the previous answer — so pre-prepared responses (or generic AI-generated ones) don't survive the follow-up. Architecture diagrams are drawn from a pool and selected randomly per session. Forward-only navigation prevents revision once a follow-up is seen.

A candidate can certainly use AI to prepare for general interview topics, as they should. What they can't do is copy-paste an answer mid-assessment and get a passing score.

What does the free trial include?

Two complete scenarios at Beginner difficulty, in any role you choose. No credit card required. After the two attempts, your account stays active — you can review your past scores indefinitely. To access Intermediate, Advanced, or Expert difficulty, you subscribe.

Are scores defensible enough to use in real hiring decisions?

Every score is produced against our published rubric. We score across three dimensions — Technical Depth (40%), Communication (30%), and Decision-Making (30%) — on a 1–10 scale with explanations for each. Hiring managers see the candidate's answer side-by-side with the model answer and percentile ranking.

We don't give you a hire / no-hire signal. We give you a defensible, explainable score you can use alongside the rest of your process.

Do badges carry weight with recruiters?

Every badge has a public verification link showing role, difficulty, score, and percentile. It's not another multiple-choice certificate — it's a signal that a candidate reasoned through a real attack-path scenario scored by a transparent rubric. Recruiters can click the link and see exactly what was tested.

Is my data and candidate data private?

Yes. Primary data is stored in India (Mumbai region). AI evaluation uses Anthropic's Claude API (US-based) — candidates see an AI-disclosure banner before starting. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. B2B candidate assessment data is isolated per account. GDPR and India DPDPA 2023 rights (export, deletion) are supported.

How long does a scenario take?

Depends on difficulty. Beginner scenarios take 5–8 minutes. Intermediate 10–14 minutes. Advanced 12–18 minutes. Expert 15–20 minutes. A full B2B hiring assessment (2–3 scenarios combined) typically runs about 30 minutes end-to-end.

Do you support voice answers?

Yes. Voice dictation is available in supported browsers (Chrome, Edge) with real-time transcription. Candidates can switch between typing and speaking freely. Voice-only mode is available as part of the premium Interview Mode add-on.

Can I integrate with our ATS?

CSV export is available on all plans. Webhooks and Zapier are available on Team Pro. Direct API access (Greenhouse, Lever, Workday-compatible) is available on Enterprise. For immediate integration needs, CSV export covers the most common workflows.
