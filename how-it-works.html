How it works

A real attack, in ~30 minutes

No multiple choice. No fluff. Four steps from role selection to a defensible, transparent score.

01

Choose your role

Pick from 15 specialized cybersecurity tracks — from Cloud Security to Red Team to AI/LLM Security. Hiring managers can pick multiple tracks for the same candidate.

02

Face a real architecture

A random cloud or enterprise architecture loads with an attack chain mapped to MITRE ATT&CK. No two sessions are identical — every retake gets a different architecture from the pool.

03

Answer 5 adaptive questions

Our AI generates follow-ups based on what you just said. Pre-prepared answers don't survive the second question. Neither does memorization or copy-paste — both are blocked at input.

04

Get transparent scoring

Scores across five anchored dimensions, side-by-side with the model answer, qualitative band, percentile ranking, and a shareable badge. Every score is auditable down to the 3-pass evaluation trace.
