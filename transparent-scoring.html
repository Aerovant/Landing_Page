Transparent scoring

Five anchored dimensions — zero black boxes

Every answer is evaluated by our 3-Pass AI engine — Evaluator, Challenger, Reconciler — against a published rubric with concrete written anchors at every score band. Candidates see exactly how they were evaluated. Hiring managers get defensible, explainable scores.

Dimension 1

Threat Identification

Identifies attack vectors, techniques, and threat actor behavior — with precise MITRE mapping at top scores.

Dimension 2

Containment & Response

Minimizes blast radius while preserving evidence — compliance-aware (CERT-In, GDPR, DPDPA).

Dimension 3

Architecture & Blast Radius

Maps architectural interconnections and impact — surfaces weaknesses and suggests segmentation.

Dimension 4

Communication Quality

Clear, structured, audience-appropriate — from board briefing to technical deep-dive.

Dimension 5

Framework Application

Correct application of MITRE ATT&CK, NIST, CIS, ISO 27001 — and knows when they don't apply.

Each question weights these dimensions differently — a "map this to MITRE" question weights Threat Identification at 40%; an "executive briefing" question weights Communication at 50%. The full weighting matrix is in our published rubric.

The 3-Pass AI Engine

Pass 1

Evaluator

Scores the answer against the anchored rubric with full per-dimension justification.

Pass 2

Challenger

Adversarially reviews the Evaluator's reasoning and challenges any unsupported scores.

Pass 3

Reconciler

Resolves disagreements with a defensible final score — the version you and the hiring manager see.
